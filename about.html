After completing a a film and video program at Georgia State Universtiy, Victoria returned to the place she interned in college -- People Store Talent Agency. After beginning her career in the with People Store in 2010 as an intern from Georgia State University. After completing her Bachelor’s of Arts in Film/Video, and two months of traveling, she was welcomed as People’s Store’s office manager, and has since made the transition to the company’s Film and Television department.
